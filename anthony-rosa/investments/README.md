# Investments

Anthony Rosa's personal investment tracking — thesis docs, position trackers, decision logs. This is the **personal layer** (`#owner/anthony-rosa`), distinct from anything Optimus does as an LLC.

## Scope

This folder holds:
- **Thesis docs.** Why a position is held — the conviction rationale, the catalysts being watched, the disconfirming evidence that would force a re-evaluation.
- **Position trackers.** Current holdings, entry prices, target prices, daily-tracking notes for active positions.
- **Decision logs.** When/why a buy, sell, or hold decision was made — including the swap from BTC profit into AKT (April 2025).

This folder does NOT hold:
- Optimus product strategy or revenue (lives in `Optimus Inc/`).
- Personal automated revenue projects (lives in `anthony-rosa/projects/` — those are projects that *generate* income, distinct from these files which track *invested* capital).
- Career skill development (lives in `anthony-rosa/skills/`).
- Daily journal entries (lives in `anthony-rosa/journal/`).

## Files

| File | What |
|---|---|
| `crypto-thesis.md` | Overall investment philosophy — risk tolerance, allocation framework, the multi-cycle horizon, the decentralized-compute thesis that grounds the AKT position. |
| `akt-tracker.md` | Active position: 59,000 AKT. Entry, current value, price targets, daily-tracking discipline, the Akash Network thesis. |
| `bitcoin-log.md` | Historical: the BTC position that funded the AKT swap. Entry, exit, decision rationale. |

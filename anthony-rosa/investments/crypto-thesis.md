## Suggested skeleton

- **Time horizon.** Multi-cycle (5-10+ years). Not trading. The position-trading window is shorter; the conviction window is the cycle.
- **Allocation framework.** What % of net worth lives in crypto vs. equities vs. cash vs. business reinvestment. Why.
- **Conviction tiers.** Which positions are long-term core (held through drawdowns), which are tactical (sized smaller, exited on thesis breakage).
- **Decentralized-compute thesis.** Why AKT specifically — the supply-side economics, the demand catalyst as agentic deployments scale, the Greg Osuri operator quality. (Cross-link to [[../north-star]] for the founder-vision framing of the same thesis.)
- **Risk management.** Position sizing rules, drawdown thresholds that trigger re-evaluation, conditions that would force exit (regulatory, technical, team).
- **Disconfirming evidence.** What would prove the thesis wrong? Naming this in advance is what separates conviction from cope.
